About Us

Pat's Salmon cookie was established on 1988 at Atlanta Georgia. Owner and founder Pat moved to Seattle from Georgia on 1995 and brought his home made recipies with him.
The first store in Seattle was established in Tacoma and Pat has been spreading the wonderful cookies around Washington ever since. This is a home owned business and we thank our customers for loving us and our cookies.
